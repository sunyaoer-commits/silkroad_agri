Typical Shipment Timeline

Kostanay elevator → Yinchuan processor

DAY 0
Contract signed & warehouse receipt pledged
SilkRoad Agri confirms lot allocation. Buyer LC opened at CCB Astana. Elevator instructed to earmark stock.
DAY 1–3
Pre-shipment quality inspection
SGS inspector takes samples at elevator. Tests: purity, oil content, moisture, mycotoxins, pesticide residues. Bilingual QR certificate issued.
DAY 3–5
KTZ wagon allocation & loading
Kazakhstan Temir Zholy allocates covered grain wagons. Phytosanitary certificate issued by KZ Ministry of Agriculture. Goods loaded.
DAY 5–7
Customs export declaration filed
GTD (customs declaration) filed with KZ Customs. 0% export VAT confirmed. Documents package prepared: contract, invoice, bill of lading, phytosanitary cert, quality cert, PoO.
DAY 7–14
Rail transit: Kostanay → Khorgos border
~1,800 km rail journey. KTZ network to Almaty junction, then south-east to Khorgos. Real-time tracking provided via platform.
DAY 14–15
Khorgos border crossing & gauge change
Bogie swap from 1520mm to 1435mm gauge. Agricultural green channel: GACC inspection typically 1–3 hours. Documents checked against GACC registration.
DAY 15–18
China-side rail: Khorgos → Yinchuan
~900 km on China Railway network to Yinchuan, Ningxia. Buyer notified of ETA 48 hours in advance.
DAY 18–20
Delivery, discharge & final settlement
Goods unloaded at buyer's designated facility. Outturn survey confirms quantity. LC documents presented by SilkRoad Agri to CCB. Settlement within 3–5 banking days.

2

SilkRoad Agri presents compliant documents to CCB Astana. CCB forwards to issuing bank in China. Payment received. CCB pre-shipment loan repaid. Net trading margin retained.

LC document checklist — what buyers must provide
01
Commercial invoice
In English and Chinese. Must match LC amount ±2%.
02
International railway bill (SMGS)
Original SMGS rail bill of lading, showing goods loaded at KZ origin station, consigned to Chinese destination.
03
Phytosanitary certificate
Issued by KZ Ministry of Agriculture. Valid 21 days from issue date.
04
Quality certificate (SGS/BV)
Third-party lab report: purity, oil content, moisture, mycotoxins. Bilingual with QR code.
05
Certificate of origin (Form CT-KZ)
Issued by KZ Chamber of Commerce. Confirms 100% Kazakhstan origin. Required for preferential duty rate.
